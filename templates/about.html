About Us

“Our mission is to bake gourmet goodies that inspire joy, foster community, and enrich the life of every customer with warmth and sweetness.”.

Durban born-Keisha Ramdhani started Unicorn Chef Bakery as a home business during Covid-19 lockdown in 2020. Just after completing her chef training at Beverley Hills hotel in Umhlanga, the world was struck by COVID-19 and lockdown. Keisha was left unemployed. Out of necessity and no job prospects when lockdown eased, she started baking professional quality, tasty baked goodies, and designer cakes to earn an income. Advertising on social media platforms brought in many orders. With her quality and tasty products and designer cakes she quickly gathered a huge following. The business started growing exponentially and the home became too small to fulfil all the orders that were coming in. Keisha took the next bold step and rented a shop with adequate space at Cornubia Mall. Through her dedication and hard work, the busy continues to grow from strength to strength. She now employs a team of 6 to assist her. She has never forgotten her humble roots and continues to give back to her community through her social investments and drives.
